About Us

At Brew Haven Coffee, we are passionate about creating the perfect cup that brings people together. Our journey began with a love for rich, aromatic coffee and a desire to share it with our community. We carefully source high-quality beans and craft each brew with care to deliver freshness and flavor in every sip. Whether you're here for a quick caffeine fix or a cozy moment of relaxation, our goal is to make every visit a warm and satisfying experience.

Coffee Menu

  • Cappuccino

    is an espresso-based coffee drink traditionally prepared with steamed milk, including a layer of milk foam.

  • Mocha

    It is a type of latte with the addition of a chocolate flavor, creating a balance of coffee's richness and chocolate's sweetness.

  • Americano

    a coffee drink made by diluting a shot of espresso with hot water

  • Frappé

    A blended iced coffee drink with coffee, ice, milk, and often sugar or other flavors, creating a slushy consistency.

  • Dalgona Coffee

    it is made by whipping equal parts instant coffee, sugar, and hot water into a thick, creamy foam

  • Matcha Latte

    is a tea-based beverage made by whisking matcha powder with hot water to create a concentrated green tea base, which is then combined with steamed milk or a milk substitute.
